Transport

An app for the Corvallis Transit System (CTS) that provides up-to-date information about bus routes, stops, and arrivals. Designed to give the people of Corvallis, Oregon better access to bus information. The key feature of this app is the inclusion of real-time bus information in an easy to digest format. Features include: Real-time bus arrivals, nearby bus stops, route maps, and route sechedules.
